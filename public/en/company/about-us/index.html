The Cryos approach

For more than a century, the conventional thinking was that immobilizing the foot was the best way to effectively correct feet and postural dysfunction.

At Cryos, we think differently

We believe that:

  • movement is essential to a healthy lifestyle
  • the feet are the foundation of a healthy posture
  • disciplined measurement is fundamental to effective treatment
  • technology is the key element to moving forward

Mission

Every day is fuelled by our willingness to help postural health professionals and their patients overcome the challenges they face. By developing integrated, innovative, high-performance solutions we contribute ultimately to help everyone rediscover the pleasure of an active lifestyle through postural well-being.

Values

Innovation

Innovative thinking permeates the development of our technology & organization towards well-being.

Transparency

We believe that transparency is the cornerstone of any long-lasting relationship.

Partnership

We join you in your efforts so that we can better understand the needs of postural health professionals and patients.

Devotion

Our devotion is a reflection of our commitment and our loyalty.

How we got here

2017
  • 2 design awards for the CryoScan 3D in partnership with Brio Innovation.
  • Close to 50 employees and more than 30 partners clinics.
2016
  • Exhibitor at the world congress of podiatry
  • New financial partner (CCPME- Desjardins capital de risque)
  • 2 new patents pending
  • Ici on recycle certification
2015
  • Hiring of a second engineer.
  • Opening of the 1st clinic in Ontario.
  • Finalist in the Fast Runner program of the Canadian trade commission in Boston.
2013
  • Opening of the new laboratory to expand our work environment of 14 000 square foot.
2012
  • Hiring of the first full time research engineer in the R&D department
2011
  • Beginning of automation of the production processes
2010
  • The company adds consulting services to help podiatrists benefit from its expertise in setting up and managing modern clinics.
2006
  • The company obtains ISO 13485 certification for the manufacture and sale of the CryoVizion system and begins marketing to podiatric professionals.
2004
  • An experienced management team is put in place to support podiatrists Gremillet and Legaré. The effects of this new alliance are felt very quickly and R&D investments accelerate the development of the dynamic contact orthotics and the CryoVizion postural imaging system.
2003
  • Following the sudden passing of Mr. Perreault in 2003, Frédéric Gremillet, DPM and Philippe Legaré, DPM carry on the founder’s vision. Both graduates of the Pennsylvania College of Podiatric Medicine and protégés of Mr. Perreault, they took over the reins of the company in 2004.
1994
  • Cryos Technologies Inc. is founded by visionary podiatrist Ronald Perrault, who develops a dynamic contact orthotic in collaboration with the Université de Montréal.
