CryoVizion imaging system

See it more clearly with CryoVizion postural imaging

Cryovizion's steps

CryoVizion is a totally non-invasive imaging solution that uses skin surface mappings for postural assessment.

Cryovizion's steps

1

You enter an observation booth where cameras capture the reflections of light projected onto your entire body.

Cryovizion's steps

2

CryoVizion software produces coloured topographic images that show your musculoskeletal system in relief, allowing the podiatrist to better evaluate and measure your condition.

Cryovizion's steps

3

Now you are able to see and understand your postural condition thanks to the images generated by the CryoVizion system and the detailed explanations from your podiatrist.
