Gain a clear and complete view of your patient’s postural condition from a single image

CryoVizion’s clear and simple images help your patient better understand their unique postural problems and the corrective action if required.

Visualize your patient’s progress

CryoVizion allows you and your patient to objectively track their progress. Compare their initial assessments with post-treatment evaluations and provide documented results of their treatment’s effectiveness overtime.

Develop trusting and loyal relationship with your patients

Validated with more than 20 years of clinical use, give your patient confidence with Cryos’ scientifically proven technology.

A credible and structured therapy

Step 1

The patient enters an observation booth where two banks of LED lights project a bright light and capture a set of images. The imaging system then captures the patient’s full body in various positions.

Step 2

From the images obtained, the CryoVizion software produces colored topographies of the skins’ surface. This allows for surface variations caused by postural, structural and musculoskeletal pathologies are easily observed.

Step 3

Using the filtered images, the clinician communicates the results of the evaluation, providing the patient with a complete diagnosis and treatment plan.

Step 4

Once the recommended treatments have been carried out, the clinician conducts a follow-up visit with the patient to take additional images and to assess the progress. At this point, any additional, necessary adjustments can be made. The follow-up images are saved in the patient file documenting the history of the patient in real-time.

Postural Evaluation By Imaging
Health professionals use imaging devices and systems for a variety of medical purposes. A validated and recommended approach used by some foot doctors is to use imaging systems to more accurately evaluate and measure postural and biomechanical problems. The visual nature of this technology allows patients to more easily understand the mechanics behind their condition and its impact on their posture. WHAT IS POSTURAL IMAGING? Postural imaging is a measurement tool that helps with the diagnostic process by providing objective data in real time.
